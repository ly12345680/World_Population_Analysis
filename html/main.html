World Population.
Analysis

The world population is the total number of humans currently living on Earth. As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, the global population was over 7.9 billion.Population growth is a critical factor in world population analysis. It is influenced by birth rates, death rates, fertility rates, and migration. Understanding these factors helps predict future population trends.
